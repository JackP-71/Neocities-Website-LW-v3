> lost in the void...

Release Date: 26th December 2025.

Created Using:Ren'Py, Gacha Life, Aseprite.

background info for the project: i decided to create this VN after discovering two characters on my Gacha Life save file (which i hadn't opened in over 5 years). i wanted to try and give these two characters a backstory, and so, this VN exists now!

this project is the first visual novel (using ren'py) i have attempted to create!

synopsis for the game: As a scientist, you are tasked with finding out more about an unknown location known as 'the void'.

Whilst completing your research, you stumble across a way to find out some information. You manage to contact twin sisters, who seem to be lost in the void, in an attempt to complete your research about the void, you begin talking to them. Initially, there is a problem, they can't see you, even though you can see them?

As you discover more you realise that they are on a mission of their own. So, at the mention of a new, potentially undiscovered planet. You are left with two choices, either heading to the planet or leave with the new found information about the void.
