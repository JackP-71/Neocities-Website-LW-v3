> my stories!

i've been writing stories for a little while now, i started writing my first 'proper' story in 2019, it was only around 5500 words in length, but it was a start.

i honestly don't even know what motivated me to begin writing.. but i wrote a first draft of one story which i aim to improve one day... after this, i decided to try and write a story about another idea i had. this one reached about 55,000 words on it's first draft and at first i was wondering how it was possible to write that much. but i really enjoyed it and was relatively happy with how it turned out although the first draft was probably lacking some of the things which make a story good.

after taking a creative writing as part of some extra curricular activity i did, which was actually quite good... although i think it was geared toward poetry... which is something i don't think i'm very good at.I still enjoyed it and it made me return to some of the idea i had... but then i remembered that i had exams... so it kinda didn't really work out... maybe when i don't have exams to worry about, i'll return and finish one of these!

it also wasn't until much later that i realised that there were potentially some background things in life that also caused these stories to happen... i made a note of them and to be honest, it kinda makes sense ^^;

anyway, fast forward to now, here we are, on a random page on the internet... where you could actually read some of this junk. sorry for rambling... but i wanted to fill this space somehow...

to be honest, i'll need to gain the courage to actually upload my stories here... so it might be a little time after a story is finished before you see it here... i'm still unsure whether i'll upload the full versions of all the stories i write... because of anxiety and all that crap.

since i wrote it, i'll make a note and say none of the stories i write contain swears.. if they do, i'll put a note for it or something..
