> Maisie.

'That's a bit weird, even for you'

> bio.

Maisie may not be the main cause of bullying at Hazelwood, but she definitely helps out when she gets the chance.

Why Maisie became a school bully is kind of unknown, it may have been something at home which lead to it, or maybe it was something at school.. either way, she's now one of the widely known school bullies at Hazelwood.

Originally, there were 3 main bullies at Hazelwood: Evie, Phoebe and Maisie. However, this all fell apart throughout the course of one week when ultimately Phoebe stopped and a couple of days later, despite feeling better, Evie stopped too.. so now Maisie is the only school bully to remain..

it's probably likely that Maisie will keep up the bullying due to her lack of interest when it comes to her schoolwork.. it's probably the one thing which interests her... she isn't really interested in all that final grade nonsense..

which she probably should be... everyone probably cares about their grades in some way after all..

> friends:

  • Evie - Evie and Maisie were best friends right up until a certain point, where Evie's illness kinda ruined their friendship.. Maisie couldn't place her finger on it, but something had changed.. and she wasn't quite sure what it was but whatever it was, she wasn't a big fan..
  • Phoebe - Their friendship isn't too great, especially as Phoebe didn't seem to enjoy the bullying that Maisie and Evie planned.. they don't really speak to each other anymore and even when they do, it usually leads to some kind of argument..

> enemies:

  • Iris - Since Iris was Evie's main enemy, Maisie also helped with the bullying.. The rivalry began almost as soon as Iris joined Hazelwood and Evie told Maisie their next target had been found.
