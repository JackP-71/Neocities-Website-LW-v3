> Amber.

'She could be in the library. If she isn't there, then I have no idea where she could be.'

> bio.

Amber loves to write. whether it's stories or plays, tragedies or comedies, she'll basically write anything. she enjoys reading and also watching theatre productions... she hopes to be a screenwright one day... she did write one play, however she wasn't too happy with it... she's kind of hoping she doesn't fall into the trap of doing a million redrafts to get it right...

One of the ideas for her stories came about when she caught wind that Iris was going on an adventure to try and make something right.. although what it was, was a bit unclear..

In fact, Amber was one of the people who helped Iris piece together the story when she was writing it.

Despite writing a few fictional stories prior, Amber never thought she'd see anything like she saw in the events of One Eye, One Girl. it did give her an idea for a story, but she ultimately left it when Iris wrote her account of the adventure.

who knows... maybe she'll be able to publish one of her works soon..

In fact, Amber probably wouldn't have even been involved with this if she wasn't friends with Beatrice, which probably roped her into Iris's antics.
