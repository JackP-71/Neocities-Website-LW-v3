> Willow.

'I should be the one who's sorry.'

> bio.

Willow doesn't say all too much, so it is a little difficult to mention things about her.

she's probbly one of the more quiet students at Hazelwood, maybe one of the quietest... but who knows..? There have been points where her friends have wondered why she's so quiet, but they shrug it off. So it's anothing thing which isn't really known.. maybe it's something to do with her past..

Willow typically keeps to herself and only really speaks with very few people. Mainly her two friends Beatrice and Iris. She also talks occasionally to Amber, but that's really only to gain some ciriticism as to what she's written. Willow loves to write, she loves to write stories and essays for some random topic for schoolwork, but she's also begun writing poetry. She's not been doing it as long as her long form writing and since she doesn't really talk about it often, it's uncertain whether she's enjoyed it.. although she has written about 6 poems so far..

How she became friends with Beatrice and Iris is kinda a mystery, but she was introduced to Beatrice first, then Beatrice introduced her to Iris... which i guess means, she suppposedly became Iris's friend out of pity... Iris was quite thankful for that though.. especially because of all the bullying she had been through.

> friends:

  • Beatrice - One of the first friends Willow made at Hazelwood, she got quite lucky that Beatrice just randomly started talking to her.. otherwise she probably wouldn't have made friends with her. Beatrice was Willow's only friend for a little while when she joined Hazelwood.
  • Iris - Willow became friends with Iris after Beatrice introduced her. They're ok friends despite not having that much in common.
  • Amber - They're mainly friends through their shared love of writing, they occasionally share feedback on each other's writing. They're quite good friends,

> enemies:

  • Willow doesn't really talk to that many people.. so she doesn't really make any enemies..
