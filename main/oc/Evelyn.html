> Evie.

'Don’t you want to win? Or are you letting me win?'

> bio.

There may have been a point in time where Evie was a nicer person... before she changed for the better..

When Evie joined Hazelwood School, she almost instantly gained notoriety for being a bully.. which is probably not the type of reputation you want to have,, but Evie didn't seem to mind.. Despite this, she managed to make friends with Maisie and Phoebe, who were somewhat fans of misbehaving too.

She spied fresh blood when she saw a new girl, who had joined Hazelwood. The new girl was called Iris.. Evie thought it was a little ironic considering she only had one eye.. Because of this, Evie decided that she would be the one she would try to torment next..

this went on for a few months, when she thought she had won when she overheard Iris and a few of her friends had talked about leaving school..

The weird thing about this was that Evie should've been feeling happy, especially on the day they said they were going to leave.. but instead she felt ill..

This was quite different to the usual evil self Evie was used to.

thankfully this problem was solved about a day later, but it did scare her friends... or maybe only Phoebe... Maisie didn't really seem to care..

> friends:

  • Maisie - They are great friends, they tend to plan the pranks together. although after the events of One Eye, One Girl.. their friendship began to falter,
  • Phoebe - idk if this counts as spoilers... but here goes.. Phoebe is said to not really enjoy the bullying, which makes sense because no one loves that, she just kinda had to put up with it... however, she did care a little when Evie fell ill.
