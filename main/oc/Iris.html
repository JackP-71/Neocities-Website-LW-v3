> Iris.

'Open your eyes everyone.'

> bio.

Iris used to be defined by the fact that she only had one eye. It was some kind mutation when she was born.

This, rather annoyingly caused conflict with every school she had been to. She had been to two schools prior to visiting hazelwood and each one she had been bullied almost constantly... originally, hazelwood was no different.. especially with the often run-in with the school bully Evie. and a few of her friends.

Since Hazelwood was the third school Iris had to go to, she was getting really bored of all this bullying nonsense. When she discovered that there was potentially a way to stop the bullying, she decided to try it.

to put a long story short, it initally created more problems than it solved... however all eventually went to plan.. i'm gonna spoil it otherwise... go read it if you want to know about it!
