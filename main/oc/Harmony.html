> Harmony.

'I'm not introducing myself to a stranger.'

> bio.

an aspiring scientist.. even if she is lost in the void. Harmony is probably going to be the reason she and Dissonance manage to escape the void.. because of the amounth of research she's doing in hopes that she'll find signs of civilisation on any of the planets they visit... so far it's only happened once or twice... and it wasn't really right.. well.. it didn't really add up..

as it stands, Harmony hasn't found signs of civilisation on any of the planets they've visited yet.. (unless you count that one rather peculiar time..) although she believes that she'll find it soon hopefully she does! Secretly, Harmony doesn't want to ever find it because of her anxiety when meeting new people... and civilisation is said to be full of people.

when she's away from her research she likes to sing, although they typically have self-depricating or melancholic lyrics. sometimes Dissonance will join in too!
