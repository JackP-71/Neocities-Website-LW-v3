> bio.

Eliza is the eldest of the triplets. with her being the eldest, you would expect her to be a 'role model' to her two younger sisters.. but she doesn't take that role too seriously.. although.. despite this, Ebony still looks up to her regardless..

She's a lot more social than ther two sisters, especially Ellie, in fact she occasionally has to talk for Ellie in some cases.. bascially Eliza is definitely the most talkative out of her two sisters.

Ebony seems to love the tales Eliza likes to tell.. since she's quite imaginative, she can think of any sort of tale on the spot.

Despite this though, she never seems to write down her tales, instead hse prefers to draw them.

she was a little tempted to start a diary considering both her sisters had written diaries.. but she wrote one entry and gave up.. maybe it was due to Ebony's disappearance.. maybe now it's resolved she'll try to write more entries..
