> Phoebe.

'i have the feeling she's learnt from the error of her ways,'

> bio.

Phoebe used to be one of bullies at Hazelwood. However, unlike Maisie and Evie, she ended up not really liking it as much..

Phoebe seemed to be a good student at Hazelwood, getting her homework done on time, completing all her work to a good standard that kind of thing.. that was until she met Evie. how this even occurred i'm not too sure.. beacause Evie doesn't seem to be a very likeable person.

When this happened, her grades seemed to drop and she no longer seemed to pay much attention to her assignments.. going from handing it on time to a good standerd nearly all the time to barely doing it at all.

This was probably one of the signs she was turning into one of the rebellious students teachers hate.

Rather fortunately, thias changed when Evie fell ill and she realised that she would have to befriend one of the people who she bullied the most to change things. That person, of course, being Iris.

Upon taking time away from Evie, Phoebe realised that maybe things had gone a little bit wrong... looking at if from afar, she weanted those good grades after all. So, she set her mind on rectifying everything that had gone wrong even if that meant become friends with the person she had been bullying for the entire time she had been at Hazelwood for. Of course, this was after they had saved Evie from whatever illness ridiculed her.
