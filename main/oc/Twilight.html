> Twilight.

'Normally, I would have to think of the characters I was going to write about, however this time, things would be different, I already know the characters in this story, I’ve met them all before.'

> bio.

Twilight is an aspiring writer. Though none of her books were ever published, she had some success uploading to a website where she gained quite a fanbase, eager for her next book or an update to an existing installment..

this went well for about 2 years before it all came crashing down when, somehow, someone in her school found her writing, it wasn't too bad, but then they connected the username to the person.. and that's when it went a little wrong..

She mainly stayed to herself during this time.. only really talking to her younger sister Raven and her friend Ella, the other students at her school just wanted to ask questions... but it kinda spiraled out of control..

Upon Ella's suggestion, Twilight moved school due to all this. This went without issue, that was until one day when the village became the problem.. not the school.

thankfully they didn't find out about her writing, but it did raise another problem.. an account she wrote on this said that the village leader (or that was what they called themselves), placed a tine limit upon their lives and when that time reached zero. Well... how can I put this? The end had been reached.

she was then determined to put an end to this.. she was especially spurred on when she realised that her sister had a really short time limit.. what with her birthday coming up.. she knew she had to do something.
