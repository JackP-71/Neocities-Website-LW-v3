Eleanor, or Ellie as she is sometimes called be her sistsers is the second youngest of the triplets. since she prefers being called Eleanor.. that's what we'll call her for the purposes of this bio.

Eleanor is typically much more melancholic when compared to her sisters. She's a lot more focussed on her schoolwork.. Hell.. even when Ebony when missing, she was still contemplating whether she should spend her time revising for the end of year exams.

She, like Ebony, keeps a diary, which she doesn't tend to update very often, but when she does, it's usually an entry about school work.. she also tends to talk about two other students in her class.. maybe it's some kind of competition Eleanor thinks goes on between them. Talking about who's getting higher grades by the end of the school year..
