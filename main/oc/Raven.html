> Raven.

'I want to live for longer, I can’t believe it'

> bio.

Raven is Twilight's sister. To some people, she's silent.. but others mainly people she knows well she'll talk a little to them. Maybe it's because of this she hasn't made many friends.. Even then she still prefers to communicate by passing notes back and forth to someone.

Raven's still discovering her passions, usually she loves to read her sistsers writing and give her some corrections although she feels it's a bit rude, even if Twilight permits it...

One thing Raven wasn't expecting near her 12th birthday, was that the person in charge of the village was placing a supposed time limit on the citizens lives. This caused Raven to isolate herself as it essentially means that she had a few days left to live.

Thankfully with the help of Twilight and Ella, the three were able to stop the time limit before Raven's time limit ran out.

> friends:

  • Ella - one of the only people Raven knows outside of her family, they aren't really on speaking terms.. but Ella understands that and doesn't push it much. They do get on relatively well despite this.

> enemies:

  • Twilight - Raven's sister, probably one of the most important people in her life. She helped Raven during the time following the announcement, it made Raven feel better knowing she still had her sistser and her mother another important person in her life looking out for her.
