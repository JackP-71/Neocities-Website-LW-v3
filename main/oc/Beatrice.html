> bio.

Beatrice is probably known as the social butterfly at Hazelwood School, she's friends with the majority of students. She spends most of her time talking to Iris, Amber and Willow though.

As much as she tries to keep on top of her studies, she tends to miss deadlines for homework and other schoolwork, instead she spends most of her time training for the cross country team. so far she has won 2 race out of 5 so far... she, like most of the team, wants to win as many as possible!
