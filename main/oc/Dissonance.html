> Dissonance.

'I don't think I've discovered a planet before... Maybe when I was younger I did, but I don't remember that...'

> bio.

Dissonance isn't a huge fan of all the science-y stuff her older twin sister researches... instead she much prefers watching the atmosphere on their ventures... mostly it's the same.. but when they land on a planet, she loves to explore them!

There was this one time when exploring a planet gave more questions than answers... but it was the first time in quite a while they had been to a planet, so she wasn't complaining about the exploration factor of it.

Because of the circumstances, she and Harmony are stuck on a spaceship most of the time, whilst Harmony focuses on research, Dissonance focuses on cleaning and organising the spaceship. because it can get a little messy if Harmony's research spirals out of control.. it helps to 'clear the mind' as Dissonance puts it.

she also occasionally helps Harmony think of song lyrics from time to time, owing to her imaginative nature.
