One Eye, One Girl.

This is the landing page for the first story i poured quite a lot of hours into :D

This is one of the first stories i wrote and i loved working on it, as the synopsis suggests it follows a schoolgirl bullied because she only has one eye.. thankfully, unlike the other schools she had to go to, Hazelwood School seemed to be the one place where she managed to make any friends.

It was also where she decided 'enough is enough' and decided to try and put an end to all this bullying crap.

i'm quite surprised i managed to write around 60,000 words for this story too... i wish i could write that much for college work :P but i would absolutely do it again for other story ideas :D

i was a little hesitant to upload the chapters to this, but i built up the courage and through december 2025 i uploaded one chapter a day until they were all uploaded :D

in terms of the chapters uploaded here, chapters 1 and 2 are from the 5th draft and the rest (3 to 28) are from the fourth draft... sorry if there are any inconsistencies between the two drafts ;-;
