Time is running out!

this was the first idea i got which got me into story writing! the first draft of this was really short (about 4500 words long..) since then, my long-form writing skills have improved and one day i want to give this idea the attention it deserves :)

i first got the idea about 4 years ago, but since then i've had a few more ideas so i really want to begin writing it soon :D

a longer synopsis of this story: after leaving an old village, a writer finds herself in a new village, this one seems a little weird.. not just because it's a new place.. but because of the ruling.. it's ruled by one person called the village leader who gives announcements every so often which everyone in the village has to attend, otherwise there are consequences..

however, one day they hear one announcement the protagonist and the rest of the village would have wished it not happened..

the one part of this story which may sound similar if you've read the book i've got one of the ideas from is the the writer part... but ideas have to come from somewhere, don't they?
