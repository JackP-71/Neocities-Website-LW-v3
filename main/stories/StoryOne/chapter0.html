One Eye, One Girl

Currently reading: Prologue

It all seemed to go wrong in the past. All the bullying. Insults. Although, mainly just the bullying. I knew something had to change. Which probably would have had a higher chance of happening if I was a nobody in a sea of students. Annoyingly, I was noticed. Noticed and bullied. Being a girl with one eye wasn’t the greatest. It would be good to be part of a school which doesn’t bully you every 5 minutes, that’s what it feels like, at least. Change would be inevitable, but given the current circumstances, I wasn’t expecting to be the one to enact the change. At least I had my friends to help me do it.

First, I should probably tell you what I’m talking about, so, let me tell you how everything changed. And, how it all went a bit wrong, but don’t worry, we managed to fix it!
