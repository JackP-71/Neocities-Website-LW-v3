There were three of us.

the idea for this story occurred to me whilst i was writing One Eye, One Girl. i tried to put off writing it until i finished that one.. but temptation won me over and i began planning it... it's only one draft through writing so far... so it's not too great and will definitely need improving in the future.

as for a synopsis for this story: two identical sisters realise their third sister has gone missing... with final exams coming closer and closer, and the risk of expulsion looming, the two sisters need to work together and maybe get the help of some other students to try and save their missing sister before it's too late.

even though it wasn't really planned, this story is considered a fantasy based on one setting in the story... this is revealed later... but if you're not a fan of fantasy novels... maybe give this one a miss..

more chapters to be added soon! stay tuned...

as a content warning, the current draft of this story contains themes of suicide - chapters which include/mention this will be marked with 'TW' before the chapter number. - whether or not the final draft i write will have this, i'm unsure.
