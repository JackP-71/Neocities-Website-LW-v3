something to note is that this page only covers the 1990s to late 2000s show.. not the 2016 reboot.

The Powerpuff Girls: Mojo Jojo A-Go-Go OST - The Suburbs.

if i'm being honest.. i have no idea how this obsession even started.. i think i remembered something about it upon seeing it on my youtube recommended. I think i originally watched snippets of this show when i was younger but i'm unsure whether that's actually the case.

then on youtube in 2023 or something i watched a small documentary of it and decided to try and find out more about it ^^

on youtube you could only really find the snippets of the episodes.. which is a little annoying but i did eventually find a dvd with some of the episodes, that was good, but it only had like 5 episodes on it. then i noticed that they were doing rereleases of the complete series, i really wanted it, but i didn't think they would ship it to my country ;-; but for christmas, i got the complete series (1-6), and i've really been enjoying it, as of writing this, i'm only up to series 4.. supposedly series 5 and 6 aren't that good.. but i wanna watch them to find out whether that's the case ..but the one thing i noticed youtube had was a full series of Powerpuff Girls Z.. which i wanna watch too at some point :)

then when i went into a comic shop, i also stumbled across a comic based off the series from dynamite comics, i didn't realise that i'd just found the first entry of it and that they were releasing them every month or so.. so of course i had to go and buy them all when they came out! note: not all the covers.. that would have got expensive really quickly.. i'd say these comics are really good and whilst they aren't the original ones, like deja view... which i really wanna read at some point.. they are still really good if you wanna read something related to the franchise :D

thinking about the episodes... i'd say my favourites (or the ones which were most memorable to me) were Bubblevicious, Mime for a change and Twisted Sister.

the movie was also quite good too, giving an origin story behind the characters. even though i think it was actually kind of a failure when thinking about how much money it made... but it was definitely worth a watch if you want to watch something relating to this :) this was actually the first DVD i picked up relating to this! so that's kinda cool i guess...

i suppose it's also worth mentioning the games, because, like all 2000s kids shows, the powerpuff girls had it's fair share of licensed games... i made it a goal to try and play them all.. so here's a small review for each of them.

  • Chemical X-Traction (PS1 and N64) - TBA
  • Relish Rampage (PS2 and Gamecube) - honestly, i don't know what to say about this game, initially it's off to a great start, with some great OST, and the 3D replica of townsville is also quite good... but gameplay wise... well... it's not too great. i tried to play as much of it as i could, but i just couldn't get past the mission which tasked you with getting five bags of gems to the bank... anyone who's played this game probably knows what i'm on about... but up until that point, the game was great and definitely worth playing! although there is definitely a difficulty spike which can definitely be realised...
  • Bad Mojo Jojo (GBC)... i think all games on the gbc are actually the same... so this was the one i played! - TBA
  • Mojo Jojo A Go-Go (GBA) - TBA
