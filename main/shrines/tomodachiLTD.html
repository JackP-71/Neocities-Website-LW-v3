> Tomodachi Life: Living the Dream.

I think we've all wanted tomodachi life to come to switch ever since miitopia got announced for it.. and it finally happened. From the time i'm writing this, the demo released yesterday, which i left college a little earlier than usual on the day to play :)

playing this demo has made me even more excited for the full game :D and this page will serve as a small diary for my journey with the game..

you'll also be able to click on the images to see them better ^^

so... one more thing to note before the diary entries begin... my first save file actually got corrupted... so this is the second playthrough of the demo.. which i didn't mind doing.. because why would i pass up the opportunity to play it all again :)

> entry 1 (25/03 - 26/03).

the demo has released and i decided to name my island after my 3ds island.. which i restarted about 2 times.. both of which i regretted..

so.. welcome to OCtopia island!

on the first time i played the demo.. i added my own mii.. but i decided, for this second playthrough i would add an OC.. so, for the purposes of this diary, the first mii i added was Harmony! on another note.. i didn't really change the mii designs.. as i was quite happy with how they looked before tbh.. and i want the miis to look like miis... but i did change the hair color to the gradient.. which i'm really glad they added :)

progressing further through the demo... the island looked really barren.. however, it wasn't too long before Harmony wanted a friend... so i bought in her twin sister Dissonance! thank goodness for the family tree thing nintendo added.. that's a really cool feature :D

also.. since i haven't really given my OCs canon voices.. i can kinda make them however i wanted.. so i gave dissonance the same kinda voice Crystal had in the direct..

it didn't take too long before the two of them became friends.. because Dissonance picked a good topic to talk about..kinda on brand topic for these OCs anyway.. :D

the island even had it's first broadcast.. brought to you by Harmony.. looks like the fountain just opened... which it is really weird to see the miis as angels... but hey, the entire game is meant to be weird.. so i guess it's cool ^^

i was wondering who i should add as my third mii.. then i decided to try out some of the mii maker customisation features.. so i kinda just tried a bunch of my OC miis which i'd transferred onto my system about a month prior.

i tried the mii painting option... which is way to powerful.. now, i'll note i'm not an artist.. but i can imagine some people making some insane stuff with this.. i think i saw someone make a mona lisa painting with it.. which is kinda insane for tomodachi life ^^;

..oh yeah... whilst i was playing.. i saw this option and was slightly worried.. lol

and that marks the end of the demo... where the miis are forever locked in their homes... bascially walking advertisements for a game i've already bought.. a great random day to release the demo! now i've got to focus on college work until then :)
