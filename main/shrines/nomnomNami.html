NomnomNami is a game developer who mainly makes visual novels using ren'py (and occasionally rpg maker). their tagline sums it up quite well, they 'write stories about girls who like girls'. Each game is definitely worth a go ^^

I'm currently in the process of playing all their games and have made it quite a way through their portfolio of visual novels.

i'll leave a link to their website here if you want to check it out, it's one of the main ones which inspired me to make mine :D

i discovered one of nami's first games 'syrup and the ultimate sweet' which was mainly due to some easy achievements for xbox video i stumbled across.. i didn't think much of it, but i did think it had a cool artstyle.

it wasn't until about 3 or 4 years later when i discovered BAD END THEATER through looking at someone else's wishlist on steam, it looked cool so i added it to mine, and a few months later, i decided to buy it, that along with her tears were my light were my first entries to nami's games, BAD END THEATER has stuck with me since i played it in december 2024 and her tears were my light admittedly didn't make much sense to me when i first played it, but i then played it for a second time in march 2026 and i finally understood it.

if it wasn't for BAD END THEATER, i don't think i would be part of this fandom.

i'm not too sure what else to put here, so i'll put some of the games which were most memorable to me, though this might be updated a little often as all of the games they have made so far are really good ^^

> drowning, drowning.

during my journey to play all of these games, i stumbled across a group on steam called the BAD END THEATER appreciation society, while i joined little after i discovered it, the owner said they set it up to bring together fans of nami's games - go give it a look if you're interested :), it now has 70 members (64 when i wrote this before.) i wonder if it will ever reach 100..

the main reason i bring this up is because they're probably the #1 fan of drowning, drowning which they recommended to me and i decided to play it. i wasn't too sure what to expect, but i'm really glad i did. it's an underwater adventure from the point of view of a trauma-coded protagonist: Maia, who enters the sea, with the help of Amphithoe and Maia is immediately roped into Am's antics. Whilst Maia is there, she also discovers a forest of kelp and an underwater city.. and the palace of Eudora, who wants to throw a dance party.. though there is the dark depths.. but i'm not gonna bring that up.. it's a dark place both literally and mentally i guess..

it may be the one place Maia is accepted,, but it's hard to tell as this idea hasn't really been explored.. However, in LWT and i guess this is spoilers for this,, Maia appears in chapter 8 (i think..) and makes it seem it's only a dream. so,, unless Maia has another dream like that.. i don't think we're getting drowning, drowning 2..
